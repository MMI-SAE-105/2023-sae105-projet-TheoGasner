His debut: from gore to drama

Peter Jackson was born in 1961 in Wellington, New Zealand. He discovered his passion for the cinema as a child, making short films with his parents' Super 8 camera. He was inspired by the film King Kong, which he saw at the age of nine.

Peter Jackson then took over the reins of the project, which he decided to divide into three films: An Unexpected Journey journey in 2012, The Desolation of Smaug in 2013 and The Battle of the Five Armies in 2014.
